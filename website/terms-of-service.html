Description of Service

What AIQ Provides

AIQ is a mobile application designed to help you track your cognitive capacity over time through periodic cognitive assessments. The service includes:

  • Cognitive Assessments - Timed tests with questions designed to measure cognitive abilities across multiple domains (pattern recognition, logical reasoning, spatial awareness, verbal reasoning, and numerical reasoning)
  • IQ Score Estimates - Statistical estimates of your cognitive performance, calculated using Item Response Theory (IRT) and presented on a standardized scale
  • Historical Tracking - Storage and display of your test results over time to visualize trends
  • Fresh Questions - AI-generated questions that rotate regularly to minimize practice effects
  • Periodic Reminders - Optional push notifications to remind you to take assessments (recommended every 90 days)

What AIQ Is NOT

It is critically important to understand what AIQ does NOT provide:

  • Not a Medical Device - AIQ is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical condition, including cognitive impairment, dementia, ADHD, learning disabilities, or any other health condition
  • Not a Clinical IQ Test - AIQ is not equivalent to professionally administered IQ tests (such as WAIS, Stanford-Binet, Woodcock-Johnson, or other standardized assessments) and should not be used as a substitute for such tests
  • Not for Official Use - Results are for personal tracking only and should not be used for employment decisions, educational placement, disability determinations, legal proceedings, or any other official purpose
  • Not Professional Advice - AIQ does not provide medical, psychological, educational, or professional advice of any kind

Recommended Use

We recommend taking AIQ assessments every 90 days (3 months) to track changes in your cognitive performance over time while minimizing practice effects.
